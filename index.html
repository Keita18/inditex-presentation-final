Managerial Recommendations

1. Network Structure

  • Maintain ~31‚Äì32 distribution centres
  • Prioritise Large warehouses (economies of scale)
  • Limited Small/Medium in key markets (Spain, UK, NL)

2. Flexible Capacity

  • Use 20% overtime for original fleet scenario (13 hubs needed)
  • With hydrogen fleet: Only 1 centre (Amsterdam) uses overtime
  • Lower transport costs eliminate need for widespread overtime

3. Fleet Investment

  • ‚úì Hydrogen as long-term target
  • Combustion/Automatic as interim steps
  • Delivers 45.8% cost reduction

4. Brexit Monitoring

  • Quarterly review of UK demand
  • If drop > 35%: reassess London, Leeds, Manchester
  • Plan contingency downsizing
5. Governance: Adopt scenario-based planning. Regularly review warehouse sizes, overtime activation, and fleet choices against updated demand forecasts and regulatory changes.
